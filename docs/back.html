Classic Prep

6 cups water

4 Tbsp. margarine or butter

¼ cup milk

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil water in medium saucepan.

  2. Stir in macaroni; cook 7–8 minutes.

  3. Drain. Do not rinse.

  4. Add margarine and milk.

  5. Add cheese sauce.

  6. Mix well until creamy.

  7. Serve and enjoy.

Tip: Prepare with less fat by using reduced amounts.
